Why I chose this project

"Abule" means Village. Going through life from my teenage years into my youth, I always longed for more, for better than what it already is. From watching movies of the western world, to reading books that depicted countries with working systems and then getting access to the internet and interacting with people outside my country, I knew it could be better than what we have. That a young man not chosing to get employed simply to survive isn't a fairy tale or some fantasy.

ALX, for me is an answered prayer, from the first word I heard Fred Swaniker say, to the last instruction from my admission guide, I knew I found my ecosystem. A people with hearts burning for chane and hands working to enact those changes.

This for me, is more than some other school or programming bootcamp, its a future in my present and for allm the promse it holds, I would willingly go the extra mile to get in and experience all of it. We've got only one earthly life, and I intend using mine for the betterment of my "Abule". It can be better, it will be better and I'm taking a step in the right direction.
